# Welcome to the Battle of the Orgs!


## In this game there are 6 teams:

### White Hats
### UAE (United Arab Emirates)
### NSO (Israeli Hackers)
### USA government orgs (FBI, NSA)
### North Korean 'Bad Actors'
### Italian 'Hacking Team'

## Each team has a different alignment, this relates to how they interact with the world and others. There are currently 4 alignments that coorespond with the teams listed below.
### Lawful Neutral (1) = White Hats
### Chaotic Evil (1) = Italian 'Hacking Team'
### Neutral Evil (2) = NSO, UAE
### Lawful Evil (2) = USA, North Korea

## Each team has 5 HP, 5 Reputation Points, and 1500 Time and Money Points

## Each of these teams will be given the choice of 4 actions total, but only one per turn rotation. They are listed below:
### Action 1 - Hack: Attack an enemy team for 1 HP, but beware, ally ties can get messy as the game progresses.
### Action 2 - Ally: Become diplomatic with an opposing team, you can maintain or lose reputation points through this action. For this action, you have 30 seconds to present a claim as to why the other team should let yoy ally with them.
### Action 3 - Propoganda: Present a message of propoganda to your followers, this action is not timed, so get creative! You will gain back 1 HP and 1 Reputation point, but it costs 120 Time and Money Points.
### Action 4 - Counter: You can block attack for 1 round, you must announce to the room when you counter on your turn. It costs 250 Time and Money Points.

## Additional Reactions:
### Action 5 - Another team attacked me???: Because this game is ran on local computer consoles, you will have to log attacks from other teams on you team. This does not count as a team action, you will still be able to choose and action for your turn after being attacked.
### Action 6 - Another team wants to ally???: If another team wants to ally with you, it you will have to listen to their claim and then choose if you want to ally with them.

## More on Points:
### Every team has a certain amount of points to their organization. These values will be averaged at the end of the 10 rounds and compared to the perfect score of 503 to decide the winner.
#### HP: These are health points, they will dictate whether or not you can take damage or not. If you are at zero HP, you can not be attacked, but you will be weakened ultimatley.
#### Reputation Points: These points coorespond to alignment of the team; the further you stray from you alignment through allyship with the wrong teams, the more reputation points you'll lose.
#### Time and Money Points: These points represent your organizations resources, you can use these to counter attacks and create propoganda to sway your followers.

## IMPORTANT NOTE:
### Time and Money Points are not endless, you CAN NOT gain back Time and Money Points for the whole 10 rounds, so spend wisely!
